Our story

A new school. An experienced team.

Established in 2025, My First Drive is built around one idea: that learning to drive should feel calm, structured and genuinely personal.

Who we are

My First Drive was founded in 2025. While the school is new, the team behind it isn't — our instructors bring over 15 years of combined teaching experience to every lesson they deliver.

We work across ten areas of West London: Hounslow, Feltham, Bedfont, Hanworth, Hampton, Teddington, Twickenham, Osterley, Isleworth and Ashford. Our focus is on building that coverage wider over time, taking on more instructors as we grow.

We don't work from a fixed syllabus. Every student is different — different experience, different nerves, different goals — and your lessons will reflect that from day one.

How we teach

Students consistently describe our lessons as calm, well-structured, and patient. We hear that a lot — and we take it seriously, because for many of our students, learning to drive hasn't been a calm experience before they came to us.

We don't rush. We don't sell you a fixed package and put you through it regardless of where you are. Each lesson starts from where you finished the last one, and we adapt as you progress.

We welcome beginners, drivers who've had bad experiences elsewhere, students who've already failed their test once (or more), and people who just need a few refresher lessons after time off the road. There's no "typical" student.

Female instructors

Female instructors are available on request. Availability is limited, so please mention this when you get in touch and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
