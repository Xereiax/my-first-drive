Choosing your lessons

Automatic or manual?

Both are recognised DVSA licences. The right choice depends on your goals and how you like to learn.

Automatic

No clutch. Just drive.

  • No gear changes or clutch to manage
  • Easier to focus on road awareness from lesson one
  • Great for nervous drivers or those with mobility needs
  • Typically faster to reach test standard
  • Automatic-only DVSA licence on passing
Book automatic lessons

Manual

Full control. Full licence.

  • Learn clutch control, gear changes, and biting point
  • Pass once — drive any car on the road
  • Full DVSA licence covers both manual and automatic
  • Standard choice for most new drivers
  • Our instructors make the clutch feel natural from lesson one
